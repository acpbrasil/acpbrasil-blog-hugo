Why ACP?

ACP Brasil actively works in the search for qualified nurses to integrate the ACP team in the home care area. Our team of professionals will come along with you to go through and transpose all the necessary steps in order for you to practice nursing in the United States as a qualified professional.

Check out the step-by-step in the tables below:

#1 - Requirements

#1 - Requirements

  • Advanced English;
  • Be a graduate in Nursing;
  • Have the active registration in the Board of Nursing of your State.

#2 - English proficiency test

#2 - English proficiency test

The nursing council of each state requires, for the most part, the proficiency of English. This proficiency can be assessed through two exams:

  • TOEFL (Test of English as a Foreign Language);
  • IELTS (International English Language Testing System).

#3 - What is CGFNS?

#3 - What is CGFNS?

The CGFNS (Commission on Graduates of Foreign Nursing Schools) is an institution that helps health professionals validate academic and professional credentials.

#4 - License

#4 - License

After the documents validation by CGFNS, the process of withdrawal of the license begins with the council of interest.

#5 - NCLEX

#5 - NCLEX

NCLEX is a test that American and foreign nurses must carry out to obtain their license and thus be able to practice the profession.

#6 - Immigration

#6 - Immigration

At this stage ACP will act as your employer (sponsor). The immigration stage will be developed by a partner office.

We will assist you in all these steps.
